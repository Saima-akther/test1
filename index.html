A Court of Thorns and Roses

Author: Sarah J. Mass

A Court of Thorns and Roses captivated me with its richly layered characters and immersive storytelling. Feyre, the resilient and determined protagonist, draws readers in with her courage and vulnerability. Rhysand, with his complex blend of darkness and tenderness, is impossible not to adore. The dynamic relationships, particularly the deep bond between Feyre and Rhysand, are beautifully explored, adding emotional depth.

The story itself is a masterful blend of romance, action, and fantasy, set in a world so vividly crafted it feels real. Each twist kept me on edge, making the series an unforgettable journey of love, sacrifice, and self-discovery.

"Hello, Feyre Darling" - Rhysand bby

Why Rhysand is the best MMC

Rhysand is the best male main character because he embodies a perfect blend of strength, intelligence, and vulnerability. His loyalty and deep love for his friends and his court make him incredibly compelling. Unlike typical alpha heroes, Rhysand values consent, equality, and Feyre’s autonomy, respecting her choices and empowering her rather than trying to control her. His backstory adds layers to his character, revealing a tortured yet noble soul who bears the weight of his sacrifices with grace. Rhysand’s wit, charm, and unwavering dedication to those he loves make him a truly unforgettable and admirable character.

Rhysand would be the ideal boyfriend because he's fiercely loyal, always prioritizing his partner's happiness and autonomy. He's supportive, kind, and deeply understanding, valuing open communication and trust. With a perfect mix of charm, intelligence, and protectiveness, he’s the kind of partner who lifts you up and stands by your side unconditionally.

Reviewed by Saima
